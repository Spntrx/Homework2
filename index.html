All About Gigi!

The Cat that Lived!!

Gigi was an abandoned cat that was rescued when she was merely 10 days old. She was raised and fed baby kitten milk on small barbie doll dishes. As the summer came and went, she grew and began to show healthy signs. As of right now she is 4 years old, as her birthday is presumed to be May 24th, 2016.
